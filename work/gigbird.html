Gig Bird Event Calendar

Celebrate every music event you enjoy with people.

Overview

Object

There are so many events happening during our daily life, you would see the information from news, Facebook or friend’s sharing. How to keep those interested events into my calendar or somewhere, even I haven't decided when to the event yet? The idea of a fully functional event calendar came out of our mine.

Approach

One designer, one developer, and one marketing personnel, there are three members in this project. We did the competitors research and market analysis then planned the rough roadmap. After had the imagination of the product, I started designing logo and website. The MVP is coming soon!

My Role

Though it is not my first time designing and planning a product from 0 to 1, I have more flexibility to do whatever I want in this project. As the only designer, my job is not only to make the logo or design layouts, I spent more time on sharing and discussing my idea with the marketing personnel. Besides, I took this chance to practice my UX skills and methods, which are the things I have wanted to execute for a long time.

Process

Briefing

Actually, the idea of building an event calendar came up from the other two members, and they invited me to join the project. Firstly, we shared our own stories specifically. Some of our stories were:

  • I can only get the concert information by accident, mostly on news or post sharing.
  • There is not a website collecting all music events, if I only follow one platform, I may miss some events.
  • Suddenly I know a photographic exhibition, when should I set a reminder on my Google Calendar?

Then we set the project goal and roadmap by took references from two successful products, Genius and Eagle. Genius is the world's biggest collection of song lyrics and musical knowledge, starting from RAP lyrics and did great then expand to all genre. Eagle created a new way to collect, search and organize your image files all in one place. Their marketing strategy was also a success by active interaction on official forum and testimonials.

Research

There are many platforms provides ticket sales and concert discovery services for music events, such as Songkick and Resident Advisor. We had good models for reference. However, most of the platforms in Taiwan focus on ticket sales rather than long-term relationship building on membership. The functions of notification and UI/UX design also lack for maintaining. After research on any possibilities, we all shared our mind map and ideas.

Project Management

Due to remote work, we made a great effort on keeping the record for everything, from meeting notes to any ideas. Considering to the expense, we saved files on Google Drive, followed tasks and managed project progress on Asana (free plan).

Naming

After finished the prototype, we conducted user testing and interviews with 12 users, most of them are our colleagues. During the process, our project manager notes all the feedback and bugs as tasks on Jira, and we discussed those opinions, decided how to optimize them.

Branding

Beyond the imagination, we finally launched the website on Jan 2018 with the marketing campaign. Along with multiple functions, users can add their favorite movie to a list, and also see other users’ rating. They can discuss and share their opinions with each other after watching a movie.

We already started cooperating with two theaters and you can directly book a movie ticket through VUE. And for the database, we have curated the largest movie database that contains over 350,000 movies and 2 million cast data in Mandarin. So far, the website of page views hit 100 users for the real-time and still growing.

Wireframing

It is time to introduce the website to more people. We planned a marketing campaign on Facebook, the goal is to let people know what VUE can do for them. Considering the engagement rate and affinity categories, we created a video to introduce the functions. We spent some marketing budget for the campaign and it attracted page like and increased page views on the website.
